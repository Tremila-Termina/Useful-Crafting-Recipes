# Useful Crafting Recipes by Tremila

## Overview

This datapack introduces various crafting recipes for survival gameplay.

May not be vanilla, but useful when gathering stuff.



## Crafting Table Recipes

### Additions

1. Most plants can now be duplicated with a bone meal. e.g. 1 dandelion + 1 bone meal = 2 dandelions
2. Small / Medium / Large Amethyst Buds & Amethyst clusters can now be crafted with 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 amethyst shard(s)
3. Barrels, Composters, and Chests can now be mass-crafted with logs.
4. Eggs can now be transformed in the following cycle: Egg -> Brown Egg -> Blue Egg -> 
5. Coral Blocks can now be crafted with 4 corals / coral fans
6. Corals and Coral Fans can now be transformed to each other.
7. Mushroom Blocks can now be crafted with 4 corresponding mushrooms.
8. Mushroom Stems can now be crafted with 2 red + 2 brown mushrooms.
9. Blocks of the copper family can now oxidize by 1 stage if not waxed.
10. Reintroduced recipe for Enchanted Golden Apple.
11. 2 Grass Blocks are now crafted with 1 dirt + 1 moss block.
12. 4 Honeycombs are now crafted with 1 honey bottle.
13. Large Grass / Fern are now crafted with 2 grass'es / ferns.
14. Leaves, Logs, and Saplings can now convert to counterparts of other tree variants.
15. 3 Leaves are now crafted with 1 corresponding sapling.
16. A level 15 Light is crafted with 1 torch.
17. Moss Block / Carpet and Pale Moss Block / Carpet can now convert to each other.
18. 8 Mud Blocks are now crafted with 8 dirt + 1 water bottle.
19. Mycelium is now crafted with 1 dirt + 1 red mushroom.
20. Podzol is now crafted with 1 dirt + 1 brown mushroom.
21. Nether Wart is duplicated with blaze powder.
22. Raw Minerals are crafted with 1 ingot + 8 cobblestones.
23. Raw Mineral Blocks are crafted with 1 mineral block + 8 cobblestones.
24. 8 Rooted Dirt are crafted with 8 dirt + 1 hanging roots.
25. Soul Sand and Soul Soil now convert to each other.
26. Sponges are crafted with 1 yellow wool + 4 gravel + 4 clay.



### Changes

1. Buttons now have 64x output. e.g. 1 oak planks = 64 oak buttons
2. Pressure Plates (but not weighted) now have 32x output. e.g. 2 oak planks = 32 oak pressure plates
3. Shelves now have 18x output. e.g. 6 stripped oak logs = 18 oak shelves
4. Trapdoors (but not iron ones) now have 32x output. e.g. 6 oak planks = 32 oak trapdoors
5. All stairs of stone type (andesite, stone bricks, etc) now have 6x output.
6. Bone Meals now have 64x output.
7. Hay Blocks now require 4x wheat to craft.
8. Weighted Pressure Plates now have 4x output.
9. Iron Trapdoors now have 2x output.
10. Sandstones now have 4x output.



## Smelting Recipes

### Additions

1. Glow Ink Sacs are now smelt into Glowstone Dust.
2. Raw Mineral Blocks are now smelt into Mineral Blocks, taking 9x as long and giving 9x as much experience.
3. Glass Blocks are now smelt into Quartz Blocks.
4. Glass Panes are now smelt into Quartz.
